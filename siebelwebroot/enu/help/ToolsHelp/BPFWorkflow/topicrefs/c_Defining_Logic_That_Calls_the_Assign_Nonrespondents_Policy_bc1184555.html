Defining Logic That Calls the Assign Nonrespondents Policy

Setting the Done Flag to N identifies the activity for this recipient as requiring additional attention. If the Policy duration is 14 days, and if the Done Flag is N, then the policy runs in 14 days. Siebel assigns recipients who do not reply to the first campaign to a new campaign after 14 days.
